About us

Today, our warehouses maintain the most popular building and finishing materials, electrical, plumbing, tools (more than 5 thousand items in stock), and we are also ready to supply items of interest to order.

Our company is an actively growing and positively established company that sells building and finishing materials. For more than 10 years we have been selling finishing and building materials wholesale and retail.

We offer such products as drywall, galvanized profiles, OSB, plywood, timber, plaster mixtures, putties and primers, adhesives, porcelain stoneware, paints and varnishes, finishing materials, dry building mixtures and much more. To date, new areas have been opened: Plumbing, Electrical and Power Tools. We constantly have various discounts, promotions, which allows you to buy building and finishing materials, plumbing, electrical and high quality tools at a great price. All you have to do to purchase the item you want is to call. Among our regular customers: construction organizations and repair teams, retail stores and private entrepreneurs, as well as ordinary citizens who themselves build a house or make repairs in their apartment.
