About us

Why are we traveling? We travel because we believe that life in this World is not limited to living within the four walls of an apartment, should not be spent in an effort to climb the career ladder and does not end with the birth of children...

The world is a huge planet where you can live while traveling, enjoy the beauty of nature, meet interesting people and earn money online... And this is our life!

We are convinced that when traveling, a person becomes richer, wiser and kinder. During each trip there is an opportunity to learn more about other cultures and customs, about the life, joys and sufferings of other people. The traveler expands his worldview, begins to appreciate what really has value, finds love and learns the meaning of life...
